Home

AB almander marinconsult provide services that include service part construction, advanced construction work and management services for large construction projects.

In 2011 AB almander marinconsult acquired AB Welin. AB Welin was specialized in manufacturing components for maritime purposes. As a result of this we now manufacture AB Welin products and provide service parts for components that AB Welin previously did. We ship worldwide and have a wide range of customers.

For many years AB almander marinconsult has offered their services as a mechanical workshop. We do everything from small part to large scale constructions.

Projects tend to consume their budget. We offer our extensive work experience as a service to you, as an on-site project manager or as an pre-project verification instance.

Please contact us for more information.

Service parts

As a result of AB almander marinconsult acquiring AB Welin in 2011, we now sell service parts to their products.

All original drawings are in our possession and incoming orders are manufactured according to the original specifications.

Examples of service parts that can be ordered;

  • Winches and airmotors.
  • Electric motors.
  • Suction pads for accomodation ladder.
  • Pilot equipment as ladder and platforms.
  • Wiresheavs of all dimension.
  • many more...

We are available to answer your questions about our service parts, do not hesitate to contact us.
